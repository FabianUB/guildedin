Resource Management 101
Tutorial Guide

💎 Understanding Your Resources

Gold (💰): Your primary currency for hiring, upgrading, and operations. Earns 5% weekly interest.

EXP (✨): Experience points for training adventurers and unlocking abilities. Also earns 5% weekly interest.

Share Price (📈): Your guild's market valuation. Higher prices unlock better ranks (E→D→C→B→A→S).

Guild Rank (🏆): Your standing in the market. Higher ranks provide prestige and access to exclusive content.
